What is
Shiba Bonke?



An open-source peer-to-peer digital currency, favoured by
Dogecoiners worldwide.



At its heart, Shiba Bonke is the accidental crypto movement that makes people smile! It is also an opensource peer-to-peer cryptocurrency that utilises blockchain technology, a highly secure decentralised system of storing information as a public ledger that is maintained by a network of computers called nodes. More than this, though, is the ethos of Shiba Bonke, summarised in the Shiba Bonke Manifesto , and its amazing, vibrant community made up of friendly folks just like you!
